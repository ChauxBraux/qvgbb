Quaker Valley senior Mimi Thiero has been named the 2026 Trib HSSN Class 3A Girls Basketball Player of the Year, earning the honor for the second consecutive season.

Thiero was the WPIAL's leading scorer during the regular season and continued to deliver in the postseason, scoring 30 points in a WPIAL first-round win over Keystone Oaks and combining for 55 points across two consolation game wins against Avonworth and OLSH. She led the Quakers to a 19-win season while being the primary focus of every opposing defense.

The senior forward averaged a double-double for the fourth consecutive year and will take her talents to the University of Maryland next fall.

Congratulations, Mimi. QV is proud of you.
