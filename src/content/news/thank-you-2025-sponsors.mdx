The 2025 season would not have looked the way it did without the businesses and families who chose to invest in this program. Nine sponsors came together to raise $5,950, every dollar of which goes directly to the girls.

## What sponsorships actually fund

When people ask what booster money pays for, the honest answer is: the things that turn a season into a memorable one. Tournament fees. Warmups that fit. Team meals on long road days. The seniors getting sent off the way they deserve.

## Please support the businesses that supported us

Our 2025 sponsors are listed on our [sponsors page](/sponsors). If you live in Sewickley or the surrounding area, please consider giving them your business. They showed up for our girls, and we want to show up for them.

Thank you, all of you.
