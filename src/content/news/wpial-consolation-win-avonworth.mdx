Quaker Valley rolled past Avonworth 54-35 in the WPIAL Class 3A consolation round, advancing in the bracket and keeping their season alive on the road to states.

The win set up a consolation matchup against OLSH, which the Quakers also won to punch their ticket to the PIAA state playoffs.
