After a 19-win season and a trip to the PIAA playoffs, Quaker Valley girls basketball has real momentum heading into 2026-2027.

The Quakers return a strong core from last year's roster, led by junior Keira Watson, who started every game as a sophomore and averaged 10.6 points and 4.5 assists per game. Anna Campbell, Rose Cline, and Maggie Watson all gained meaningful varsity experience and are ready to take on bigger roles.

The team will look different without Mimi Thiero, Lucy Roig, and Mia Flora, but Coach Ken Johns has emphasized the advantage of continuity and a group that already knows how to compete at a high level. The focus heading into next season will be on defensive improvement and building the kind of chemistry that carried this year's team deep into the postseason.
